# ✨ Ledger — Feature List

## 📥 Capture & Import

- 🧾 **Scan Receipt** — OCR + AI-powered field extraction from printed or handwritten receipts
- 📱 **Scan Screenshot** — AI vision reads UPI confirmations, payment-history lists, and mini-statements directly
- 📤 **Share Target** — Ledger appears in your phone's native share sheet; share a screenshot straight from your UPI app
- 📄 **Import Bank Statement** — PDF, CSV, XLS, or XLSX, up to hundreds of rows
- ✍️ **Manual Entry** — full manual form with voice input and location tagging
- 🔀 **Multi-Entry Batch Review** — every detected transaction becomes an editable row before it's saved
- ➗ **Split Rows** — break apart merged transactions detected as one

## 🧠 AI Intelligence

- 🏷️ **Auto-Categorization** — every transaction sorted into your own custom categories
- 🔍 **Document-Type Detection** — automatically tells receipts, invoices, UPI screenshots, and bank statements apart
- 🎯 **Confidence Flags** — low-confidence reads are visually flagged for a quick double-check
- 🧠 **Merchant Memory** — corrections you make are remembered for next time
- 🤖 **AI Fallback Parsing** — optional AI assist for messy handwritten receipts

## 📊 Insights & Projections

- 🥧 **Category Spend Breakdown** — week, month, or custom range
- 📈 **Spending Trajectory** — end-of-week, end-of-2-weeks, and end-of-month projections
- 💬 **AI-Written Spending Summary** — plain-English narrative grounded in your real numbers
- 💡 **Smart Saving Suggestions** — specific, category-based, not generic advice
- 🏆 **Financial Health Score** — savings rate, budget adherence, and debt load, graded S–D
- 🔁 **Subscription Audit** — auto-detects recurring charges and annualizes their cost
- 🚩 **Anomaly Flags** — highlights unusually large transactions
- 🗓️ **Full-Year Spending Heatmap**
- 💰 **Net Worth Tracking** — cash + goals − debts, over time
- 🌊 **Income → Category Sankey Diagram**

## 📋 Ledger & Dashboard

- 💳 **Transaction Ledger** with global fuzzy search, tags, and MoM deltas
- 🎯 **Goals** with contribution tracking and a rarity-bordered featured card
- ❄️ **Debt Snowball** planner with payoff timeline
- 🔄 **Recurring Transactions** with automatic catch-up
- 👛 **Multiple Wallets** with per-wallet breakdowns
- 🏷️ **Custom Categories**
- 🧩 **Draggable, Hideable Widget Grid** on the dashboard
- 🔥 **No-Spend Streaks** and a daily reminder
- 🗺️ **Personal Spending Map** (Leaflet + OpenStreetMap)

## 🎨 Personalization

- 🌗 **Light / Dark / Cyberpunk / High-Contrast** themes with a custom accent picker
- 🇮🇳 **INR-first** formatting (₹ with lakh/crore grouping) — overridable to any currency
- 📱 **Fully Installable PWA** — home-screen icon, offline support, update prompts

## 🛡️ Data Safety

- 💾 **Local-First Storage** — IndexedDB, with an automatic fallback engine on locked-down browsers
- 📤 **Full Export / Import** (JSON) with schema versioning
- 🗑️ **Trash View** with 30-day auto-purge
- ⏰ **7-Day Backup Reminder**
- 🔑 **Bring-Your-Own AI Key** — stored only on your device
